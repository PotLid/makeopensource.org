Our Mission

MakeOpenSource's goal is to create a community of programmers and developers (of any skill level!) who can collaborate and contribute to one another's projects.

Open Source for All

Just as you don't have to be a computer scientist to have an interest in programming, you don't have to be a computer science major to be a part of MakeOpenSource. If you want to strengthen your programming knowledge, MakeOpenSource's events are a great opportunity to do so.
